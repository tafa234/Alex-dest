Vacations in Alexandria

300$ - 3 Days

"Discover the Wonders of Alexandria: A Timeless Journey Through Ancient Treasures and the Sparkling Mediterranean!"

Embark on a magical 3-day getaway to Alexandria with Rahhal Agency. Dive into the rich history and coastal charm of Egypt’s Mediterranean jewel. Experience exceptional services, including comfortable transportation, cozy accommodations, and a knowledgeable guide. The journey covers iconic landmarks such as the Citadel of Qaitbay, the Bibliotheca Alexandrina, Montaza Palace Gardens, and the Catacombs of Kom El Shoqafa. Stroll along the Corniche, savor delicious seafood, and immerse yourself in Alexandria’s timeless beauty. Book your trip now for an unforgettable experience at unbeatable prices!

Day 1: Arrival in Alexandria

-Arrive, meet your guide, and transfer to your hotel.
-Visit the Citadel of Qaitbay and the Bibliotheca Alexandrina.
-Evening walk along the Corniche.
-Overnight in Alexandria.

Day 2: Exploring Alexandria

-Visit Kom El Shoqafa Catacombs and Pompey's Pillar.
-Explore Montaza Gardens and enjoy lunch by the sea.
-Free evening for shopping or relaxation.
-Overnight in Alexandria.

Day 3:Farewell Alexandria

-Breakfast and check-out.
-Visit Stanley Bridge and enjoy some leisure time.
-Depart from Alexandria.
